WHERE IS THE BUS?
Click on the map to get started

Welcome to the Portland Bus Finder App! This app uses realtime GTFS location data from Trimet busses to tell you where your bus is and when it will get to your stop. First click on the map. Then the app will return to you the five closest bus stations. When you click on a station the app will show you the line and direction of the next bus arriving at the stop. It will also tell you how long of a walk it is to the station in minutes, and how far away the bus is in minutes.

The red dot is the realtime location of the bus when you click on your stop!

Happy Travels!
